Ayrton Senna da Silva: 21 March 1960 - 1 May 1994
 

Introduction



Ayrton Senna da Silva, was born on the 21 March 1960, in Sao Paulo, Brazil. He is a motor racing driver who has won the Formula 1 World Drivers Championship on 3 occasions (1988, 1990, 1991), he has won 41 Grand Prix and has 65 Pole Positions. Senna is considered by many to be the greatest driver of all time, due to his one lap pace, his consistancy and his unique ablity to driver fast in the rain. Senna would sadly die in a Formula 1 race in Bologna, Italy. He was 34 years old, his passing was one of the most sadest days in the F1 sporting history.
